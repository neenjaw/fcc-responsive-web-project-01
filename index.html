A Tribute to INTERNETHULK

March 22, 1987 - November 8, 2017

INTERNETHULK photographed for the professional Overwatch team, 'Team EnVyUs'

Introduction

Dennis Hawelka, known to the e-sports world as INTERNETHULK, was a German player who began playing Overwatch in 2016. He became well known due to his skill and e-sports acheivements, eventually transitioning to a coaching role with Team Liquid.

Major E-sports Accomplishments

  • 2016
    • October 31 - Second place - Overwatch Open
    • December 3 - First place - Overwatch APEX Season 1
    • December 18 - First place - Major League Gaming Vegas 2016
  • 2017
    • October 31 - Fifth-Eighth place - Overwatch APEX Season 2

Legacy

Following his passing, his life has inspired many gamers to be a positive influence to the e-sports community. In 2017, Blizzard Entertainment announced the Dennis Hawelka Award to be awarded to the player with the most positive impact on the Overwatch community at the end of each Overwatch League season.
